About Battleship Game

Game Overview

Battleship is a classic strategy game where two players take turns guessing the locations of each other's ships on a grid. The objective is to sink all of the opponent's ships before they sink yours.

How to Play

  1. Players take turns guessing coordinates to hit the opponent's ships.
  2. The first player to sink all of the opponent's ships wins!

Game Features

  • Two-player mode
  • Score tracking
  • Responsive design for mobile and desktop
